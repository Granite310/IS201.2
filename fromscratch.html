Tennis with Grant

Hi, I'm Grant and I love tennis! I started playing tennis when I was 12 years old and have been hooked ever since. Tennis is a great way to stay active and have fun with friends and family. I made it all the way to the Alaska state championship in high school and have continued to play for fun now that I'm in college. I love watching tennis and am always excited to see the talents across generations clash on the court.

Why I Enjoy Tennis

  • Tennis is a great way to stay fit and a sport you can play well into old age
  • It has a nice combination of physical fitness and mental fortitude that allows you to play at high levels
  • There's something different about the way it feels to hit a good tennis ball back across the net that keeps me coming back for more
  • Tennis is a good opportunity to bring people together to socialize and have healthy fun

My Favorite Players

  1. Roger Federer
    • Federer is the considered the greatest tennis player of all time and has won 20 Grand Slam titles
    • He has a graceful playing style and I admire his sportsmanship on the court
    • He uses a 15 second reset technique in between points that I've adopted into my own game
  2. Jannik Sinner
    • Sinner is a rising star in tennis known for his powerful serve and aggressive baseline play
    • He plays with a calm air and I like how he controls the court
    • He holds himself very well and plays like I think a champion should
  3. Carlos Alcaraz
    • Alcaraz is a talented young player known for his powerful groundstrokes and competitive spirit
    • He plays with a fire that reminds me of some of my other favorite players
    • He knows how to lean into the crowd and is able to use them to create his own momentum
